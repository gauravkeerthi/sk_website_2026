When compliance isn't your job, but the risk is still yours

Does compliance matter, but it was never meant to be your job?

You know compliance matters, but it's not even what you're hired to do! Suddenly, you're handling all the policies, evidence, and answers about cyber... The issue isn't effort, it's that no one ever showed small businesses how to do this properly.

Do you feel uncertain because everything is scattered?

You're willing to put in the work, but the uncertainty is exhausting. You don't know what's mandatory, what's optional, or how much detail is required. Templates feel generic, consultants feel expensive, and uncertainty drags everything out.

Do you just want to pass, without becoming a compliance expert?

You don't want to specialise in compliance or spend months figuring it out. You just want a clear way to achieve "passed", without the endless back-and-forth. Compliance shouldn't feel like a maze, it should feel like a checklist you can complete confidently.

Here's how being compliance-ready looks like

Generate required policies automatically, tailored to your business

Create the policies you need without writing from scratch. In a single click, you can generate ready-to-use policies aligned to certification requirements, customised to how your business actually operates.

  • No need to learn policy writing or legal language
  • Review, adapt if needed, and move forward
  • Know the basics are covered from day one

Collect and organise evidence through simple, guided steps

Gather the right evidence without second-guessing what's required. With a collated checklist of required evidence files, you can see exactly what's needed, use ready-made templates to prepare it, and follow clear guidance on how to submit.

  • Everything stays organised in one place
  • No hunting through folders or guessing
  • Step-by-step guidance for each requirement

Complete compliance documentation with confidence

Complete compliance knowing that you've got everything sorted out. Documentation doesn't feel like guesswork when you've got all your policies, evidence, and controls in one place.

  • No more rewriting or second-guessing responses
  • Paperwork done clearly and consistently
  • Ready for review or submission when needed

Get all baseline cybersecurity tools needed for compliance in one place

Instead of managing multiple tools, separate dashboards, and reports in order to meet compliance requirements, you get everything you need—from internet security, staff training, device protection, and password manager—all in one place!

  • Everything visible in one platform
  • Easier to stay aligned and avoid gaps
  • No juggling multiple vendors
