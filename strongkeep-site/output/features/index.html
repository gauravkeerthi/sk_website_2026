Compliance

The Compliance feature guides you through every requirement and tracks your progress — no consultants, no guesswork. We support standards like CSA Cyber Essentials, Data Protection Essentials, and Health Information Act (soon). Plus, with our partnership with the certifying body, you'll get certified faster and with confidence.

  • Achieve certification within weeks not months
  • Fully guided process complete with helpfiles and templates
  • Streamlined efficiency using AI and automation

Protection

StrongKeep equips you with various protection tools that work quietly in the background to stop phishing and ransomware before they hit. Device protection (powered by Palo Alto Cortex Technology) and network firewall are just some of the many tools on the platform that run automatically to keep threats out and your systems safe.

  • Device protection proactively blocks threats
  • Network firewall guards against suspicious activities
  • Built-in automation for your peace of mind

Training

The Training feature provides bite-sized cyber awareness lessons and phishing simulations that equip your team with both knowledge and practical skills. No classrooms, no long lectures—just short, engaging modules that build lasting security habits and confidence. Turn your staff to become your company's strongest shield.

  • Awareness training turns best practices into daily habits
  • Phishing drills keep your team alert and ready
  • Build confident teams to defend your business from within

Detection

StrongKeep equips you with detection tools that spot weaknesses in your systems before attackers do. Don't leave open doors for hackers. Integrate the tools that check for vulnerabilities, flag risky settings, and provide actionable insights to fixing the issues. With automated scans and guided fixes, you'll always stay one step ahead.

  • Spot security gaps early with regular domain scans
  • Uncover hidden vulnerabilities in your systems
  • Follow clear, actionable steps to boost your defences

Response

Even the best defences can be tested but panic isn't part of the plan. Stay calm with the necessary tools when things go wrong—with ready-to-use playbooks, diagnostic tool to identify cyber incidents, and cyber insurance (backed by our partners Protos Labs and MSIG) for that extra layer of coverage for when prevention isn't enough.

  • Step-by-step crisis playbooks for confident response
  • Automated support for recovery and insurance claims
  • Add on cyber insurance for that extra peace of mind
